Structural Integrity

Specialized Inspection and Assessment services to evaluate performance, identify vulnerabilities, and ensure the long-term safety of your existing structures against seismic and climate-related loads.

Structural Audit & Performance Evaluation

A comprehensive deep-dive into the current health of your building. We utilize state-of-the-art diagnostic tools to detect reinforcement corrosion, concrete carbonation, and hidden structural defects that could compromise safety.

Leveraging advanced computational modeling and real-world ground motion data to simulate earthquake impacts on your structure. We follow international PSHA (Probabilistic Seismic Hazard Analysis) standards to identify failure points.

  • ✔ Retrofitting Design Plans

  • ✔ Non-Linear Static Analysis
  • ✔ Risk Mitigation Strategies
