Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide high-precision structural engineering quotes and site investigations, we collect project-specific data including:

  • Project Classification: (Structural Design, Assessment, or Retrofitting)
  • Technical Specifics: (Area, Material types, Structural purpose)
  • Site Location: Accurate project address for seismic hazard evaluation.
  • Contact Details: Professional identification, primary email, and secure phone numbers.

2. Data Utilization

Data gathered is used exclusively for engineering evaluation and drafting technical proposals. Our Internal Review Board ensures all structural inquiries are processed with the highest confidentiality.

3. Data Protection & Sharing

AAES maintains a Zero-Sharing Policy with third-party marketers. Your technical project data is routed directly to our secure administrative gateway at admin@aa-engineers.net.

4. Engineering Security Standards

We implement SSL encryption and industry-standard security protocols to protect sensitive blueprints and project documentation during transmission.

5. Professional Rights

Clients maintain the right to project data erasure. To revoke access or request data deletion, please contact our data officer at admin@aa-engineers.net.
