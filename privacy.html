Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with the AAES Chatbot on Facebook Messenger, we collect the following information to provide you with a structural engineering service quote:

  • Project Type (Design or Assessment)
  • Project Details (Area, Material, Purpose)
  • Project Address
  • Contact Information (Name, Email, Phone Number)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely to evaluate your structural engineering needs and contact you with a service proposal. All data is sent safely to our internal team.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is sent directly to our administrative team at admin@aa-engineers.net.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the information transmitted through our chatbot.

5. Your Rights

You may request the deletion of your data at any time by messaging our Facebook Page or emailing us at admin@aa-engineers.net.
