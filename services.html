Structural Engineering

Beyond stability, AAES integrates deeply into the design process to generate lasting value for a structure’s vision, cost, and legacy. We go beyond traditional engineering to align with your project goals. We leverage innovation and technical expertise to elevate both your structures and your communities.

Structural Design & Analysis

Complete analysis for all structural elements including foundations, slabs, beams, columns, and roofing systems. Calculations are optimized for wind and seismic loads to exceed safety requirements.

Deliverables:

  • ✔ Structural Calculation Report
  • ✔ Detailed Permit and Construction Drawings
  • ✔ Technical Specifications

Inspection & Assessment

Evaluation of structural performance for existing buildings. We identify defects, assess safety against extreme events, and provide clear recommendations for maintenance or retrofitting.

Deliverables:

  • ✔ Full Structural Audit Report
  • ✔ NDT/Structural Testing Results
  • ✔ Official Structural Certification

Featured Case Study

Supermarket Steel Warehouse Optimization

For this large-scale supermarket warehouse, AAES implemented an advanced Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) design approach. By optimizing the structural weight and frame efficiency, we achieved a massive 30% reduction in total material costs.

"While the cost-efficiency was significant, AAES made sure every element complies with stringent international and local structural codes, ensuring the facility remains ultra-safe and durable."
