Our Services

Here at Aceron Aquino Engineering Services, we provide structural engineering solutions.

Below is an overview of the structural engineering services we offer:

STRUCTURAL DESIGN and ANALYSIS

We provide complete structural analysis and design services for all projects. Our work covers all structural elements, including foundations, slabs, beams, columns, walls, bracings, and roofing systems. We give specific consideration for resistance against wind loads and seismic loads. Each design is developed to meet safety, strength, and code requirement.

You will Receive:
✔ Structural Design and Analysis Documents
✔ Structural Drawings

STRUCTURAL INSPECTION and ASSESSMENT

We provide structural inspection and assessment services for existing structures. We evaluate structural performance, identify defects or damage, and assess safety against wind loads and seismic loads. Our reports provide clear findings and recommendations to guide repairs, retrofitting, or maintenance.

You will Receive:
✔ Structural Inspection Report
✔ Structural Testing Report
✔ Structural Certification

STRUCTURES WE DESIGN:

• Residential Areas (Housing)
• Commercial Buildings
• School Buildings
• Office Buildings
• Retaining Walls
• Miscellaneous Structures (Posts, Support for Billboards, Support for Signages, and more)
