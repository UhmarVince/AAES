STRUCTURAL DESIGN and ANALYSIS

Structural design and analysis involve the planning of a structure's ability to withstand loads, and environmental conditions. Key objectives include:

✔ Developing efficient designs for reinforced concrete, steel, timber, and other structural systems.
✔ Assessing the impact of loads such as weight, wind, seismic activity, and other forces.
✔ Ensuring compliance with building codes and engineering standards.
✔ Identifying and mitigating potential structural vulnerabilities.

STRUCTURES WE DESIGN:

✔ School Buildings

✔ Retaining Walls
✔ Miscellaneous Structures (Posts, Support for Billboards, Support for Signages, and more)
